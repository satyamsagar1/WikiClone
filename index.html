Virat Kohli (born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is considered one of the best batsmen of his era.

Early life

Virat Kohli was born in Delhi. He began playing cricket at the age of nine and joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy in 1998.

Domestic career

Kohli first gained fame when he led India to victory in the 2008 ICC U-19 Cricket World Cup. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL.

International career

Kohli made his ODI debut in 2008 and his Test debut in 2011. He became Test captain in 2014 and led India in all formats until 2021.

  • ODI Debut: 18 August 2008 vs Sri Lanka
  • Test Debut: 20 June 2011 vs West Indies
  • Captaincy: Full-time Test captain from December 2014

Records and achievements

  • Most hundreds in ODIs (50+)
  • Fastest to 8,000 to 12,000 ODI runs
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018

Personal life

Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in December 2017. They have a daughter named Vamika.
